LEGO AT-AT

Robot Camels

For the 40th anniversary of Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back, LEGO released this work of art as a set. Featuring six minifigures, this set is not only a pretty big set, for having a 1,267 piece count, but it looks almost like it did in the movie, aside from the blocky texture you'd expect from a LEGO set.

Average pricing: $200 - $300 per set, depending on condition.
