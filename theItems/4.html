HALO: REACH SOUNDTRACK ON CD

Remember Reach

Halo: Reach is a 2010 first person shooter on paper. But upon actually playing the game, you tend to learn that games can be art. The music, courtesy of Marty O'Donnell and Michael Salvatori, is an emotional, soothing, intense, and tragic masterpiece of an album that truly aids Bungie's farewell title as not just a game, but an experience.
