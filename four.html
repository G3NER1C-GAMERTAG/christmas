FOUR: HALO: REACH SOUNDTRACK

Halo: Reach is a 2010 first person shooter on paper. But upon actually playing the game, you tend to learn that games can be art. The music, courtesy of Marty O'Donnell and Michael Salvatori, is an emotional, soothing, intense, and tragic masterpiece of an album that truly solidifies Bungie's farewell title as not just a game, but an experience.

Average pricing: $35 - $100 per copy, based on condition
